- Include specific tool/version references and limitations
- Assume project context knowledge, focus on implementation
- Explain decisions, not benefits
- `kebab-case.md` file naming (except common conventions like `README.md`, `CONTRIBUTING.md`)
- Diagrams: use Mermaid

## Avoid AI writing tropes

### Word choice
- No magic adverbs: "quietly", "deeply", "fundamentally", "remarkably"
- No "delve", "leverage" (verb), "utilize", "robust", "streamline", "harness", "genuine"
- No ornate nouns: "tapestry", "landscape", "paradigm", "synergy", "ecosystem"
- Use "is"/"are" instead of "serves as", "stands as", "represents"

### Structure
- No filler transitions: "It's worth noting", "Importantly", "Interestingly", "Notably"
- No shallow -ing tails: "highlighting its importance", "reflecting broader trends"
- No fractal summaries (intro summary + section summaries + conclusion restating everything)
- No signposted conclusions ("In conclusion", "To sum up", "In summary")

### Tone
- No pedagogical hand-holding: "Let's break this down", "Let's unpack this"
- No bold-first bullet patterns (`**Keyword**: description...`)
